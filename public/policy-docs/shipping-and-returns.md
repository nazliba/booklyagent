# Bookly — Shipping & Returns Policy

*Last updated: July 2026*

## Shipping

- **Standard UK delivery** takes 5–7 business days and is **free on orders over £25**. Orders under £25 are charged a flat £3.99 delivery fee.
- **Expedited UK delivery** (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for £6.99, regardless of order value.
- Delivery time frames begin from the date of dispatch, not the date of order — please allow up to 1 business day for an order to be processed and dispatched.
- We currently deliver to UK addresses only. Orders cannot be split across multiple delivery addresses; please place separate orders if items need to go to different addresses.

## Returns & refunds

- You may return any item within **30 days of delivery** for a full refund, provided it is in its original, unused condition.
- Refunds are issued to the **original payment method** within 5–7 business days of Bookly receiving the returned item.
- **Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility**, except where the item arrived damaged, defective, or was sent in error — in those cases, Bookly covers return postage.
- For items over £50 in value, we recommend using a tracked return service, as Bookly cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit back to us.
- Digital/eBook purchases are non-returnable once downloaded, except where required by statutory consumer rights.
- Gift cards are non-refundable.

## Order lookups & account security

To protect customer privacy, Bookly's support agent (chat, phone, or email) will only disclose order details after verifying **both**:
1. The order number (found in your order confirmation email), **and**
2. The email address used to place the order

This verification is required even if the customer is not logged into a Bookly account, and applies to every channel equally. Order details will not be shared based on order ID alone.

## Repeat returns

Accounts with more than 3 return requests within a rolling 90-day period are flagged for manual review by a Bookly support agent before a refund is auto-approved.
